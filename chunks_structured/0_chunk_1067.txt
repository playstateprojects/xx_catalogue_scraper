Saariaho, Kaija
(b. 1952)
Country: Finland
Annotations: Developed techniques of computer- assisted composition. Works with tape and live electronics. Awards: Grawemeyer Award, Wihuri Prize, Nemmers Prize, Sonning Prize, and Polar Music Prize.
