The exciting realm of recent discoveries in biology inspired Camille van Lunen, Artist in Residence at the Instituto Gulbenkian de Ciência in her new composition, Quatuor pour l'Aurore des Temps. Each movement of the work illustrates one of the mysteries of the origins of life: Cell division – Crystal for- mation - Virus attack.
