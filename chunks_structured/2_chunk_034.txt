In memoriam Gertrude Stein. Three short pieces using the sounds of the instrument, the singing and speaking voice as well as horn sounds, vocal sounds, noises and the sounds of speech. Flodigarry is the name of a small village in Scotland, a "magical place" (E. Zobel). The work was initiated by the hornplayer U. Kahl.
