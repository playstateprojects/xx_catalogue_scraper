Carr-Boyd, Ann
(b. 1938)
Country: Australia
Annotations: Composer, musicologist. University of Sydney. Studied in London with Peter Racine Fricker and Alexander Goehr. Albert H. Maggs Composition Award. Co-founder of the Lane Cove Symphony Orchestra.
