Die Lieder der Owl Woman haben heilende Eigenschaften und sind am Bett eines kranken Menschen zu singen. Setting of poems written by an American Indian of the Papago tribe, OWL WOMAN (19th century). A meditative work. The songs of Owl Woman have a healing effect and are meant to be sung at the bedside of some- one who is ill. Composition commissioned by the Scottish Arts Council for „Soundstrata", Glasgow.
